How to make a cost-effective catapult

This is done in 5 easy steps

Ingredients: Hot glue, Binder clip, 4 Popsicle sticks, Scissors

First step, cut a popsicle stick into fourths, then hot glue into a square with the fourths.

Second step, hot glue 2 popsicle sticks to the binder, clip with both hot glued to the binder clips LONG end on each side.

Third step, Hot glue the square we made earlier to the top.

Fourth step, make any reinforcements with wooden dowels and sticks.

Last step, FIRE!
